The Rice Purity Test is a questionnaire that asks you 100 questions about your life to determine your personality in terms of your education, social life, and innocence.


The Rice Purity Test is a questionnaire with 100 questions for both males and girls that covers topics like infringement, education, society, and your personal life. In other words, the Innocence Test is another name for the Rice Purity Test. It is created by the RicePurityTests team to assess a person's purity. The purity score is a number between 0 and 100. where 0 is the lowest score and 100 is the highest (a terrible score) (good score). The rice purity test was developed by Rice University in 1924 to assess students' levels of maturity and innocence in relation to their age group. The major goal of the rice purity test is to help the freshmen have a wonderful college experience and maintain strong emotional ties with their elders.

How Does it work

There are 100 questions on the test for rice purity. In the online purity test, each question about your life or what you've done with you must be answered by clicking the checkbox for yes and leaving it blank for no. Each question in the rice purity test receives a score that ranges from 0 to 100 and can be either affirmative or negative.

To pass the rice purity test, you must correctly answer all 100 questions. Therefore, don't hesitate to take the purity test because it will show you how to improve and strike a good bargain with your partner and take your relationship to the next level.

  • Click the test you want (full for 100 questions and quick for 20 questions)
  • Read the questions then click the chekcboxes for yes and leave them blank for no
  • After youv'e finished answering the questions click the submit button to get your scores and finish the test
